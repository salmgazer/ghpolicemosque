The Sharjah Charity Association donates a hearse

The Sharjah Charity Association, through the United Arab Emirates Embassy (UAE) in Accra,
has donated a hearse to the Ghana Police Mosque to aid the activities of the Mosque

We are grateful to The Sharjah Charity Association

Thanks to The Sharjah Charity Association, the mosque now has a hearse to serve the Ummah.
